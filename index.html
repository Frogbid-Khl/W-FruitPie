The NFT

Our chef, host, and barista, who simply goes by “Breakfast,” has programmatically generated a series of delicious meals in exotic locations. With over 200 unique traits available, each breakfast is served as an ERC-721 token on the Ethereum Blockchain and includes a high res image in a ~1.3:1 aspect ratio and an additional panoramic version at a 3:1 aspect ratio. The panoramic just so happens to fit as your Twitter Banner, perfect if you’re into sharing shots of your food.

The Coffee

Breakfast loves coffee and knows exactly where to get the good stuff, which is why we turned to our friends over at yesplz.coffee to create our unique blends. Yes Plz is known for roasting insanely-delicious, perfectly-balanced mixes of the world’s best coffee beans, and we’ve commissioned them to make sure the Bored Breakfast Club gets nothing but the best. Yes Plz Coffee proudly roasts in Los Angeles, CA and was recently ranked in the top 10 coffee subscriptions by NYTimes/Wirecutter.

Community Coffee Wallet Explained

The Community Coffee Wallet is funded by a royalty on secondary market sales, as well as a percentage of profits from coffee and merch sales. Once the wallet reaches a threshold that allows us to share a new blend with our Bored Breakfast Club members, we’ll drop a new blend. The funds go toward paying for packaging, roasting, fulfillment, and free shipping to our members all over the world.
